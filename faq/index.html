What is the dress code?

We recommnend colorful, formal attire. Think olives, flowers, berries, and nature colors.

Are any of the wedding events outdoors?

The cocktail hour and reception will take place inside at the Cordelle venue.

The ceremony will take place in the outside yard of the Cordelle venue.

Where should I go between the ceremony and reception?

Guests are welcome to return to their accommodations or go to one of the many bars and restaurants walking distance from the Cordelle.

Are children allowed at the ceremony and reception?

We love children, but we would like to encourage parents to enjoy an adult-only evening.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We kindly ask that there are no phones or cameras during our wedding ceremony.

Will there be parking available?

There will be street parking available at the Cordelle venue.

We recommend using a ride share option, such as Uber or Lyft, as street parking is available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Will there be vegetarian options?

Yes! Please let Chloe or Michael know about any dietary or allergy needs.
