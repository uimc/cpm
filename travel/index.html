Activities

Nashville Zoo

An amazing experience, especially when the sun is out. Defintely visit the Rhinos, Panther, Lemurs, and Flamingos!

Centennial Park

Nashville's own central park... but with a Roman building right in the middle. Be sure to admire the Parthenon if you decide to visit!

Ryman Auditorium

A great venue to see a show in Nashville! They have shows almost every night. Even if you don’t know the artist, the chances are it will be a great show.

Adventure Science Center

This place holds a special spot in Michael's childhood. Honestly, this place is still super fun, even as an adult.

Broadway

Need we say more?

Good Eats

Benji’s Bagels ($+)

Amazing bagel shop with many different flavors of schmears and bagels to choose from. Definetly a nice breakfast or lunch spot to get something to tie you over.

Cafe Babu ($+)

This coffee shop serves Chloe's favorite lattes in town. It is also very close to the wedding venue. Only stop by if you have a little extra time to wait in line... Chloe's not the only one who loves this place.

Hattie B’s ($+)

Most out-of-towners go to Hattie B’s for authentic Nashville hot chicken... and there's a reason why!

Martin’s BBQ Joint ($$)

Our favorite BBQ restaurant. Chloe recommends their brisket tacos, and Michael recommends (trust me) their bologna sandwich if you’re feeling adventurous.

Ingrassia & Sons Deli ($$)

A true Italian deli. You can't go wrong with any of their sandwiches, so we recommend them all!

Thai Ni Yom ($$+)

This is our favorite restaurant, period. We eat here so regularly that the server knows our orders. They serve delicious Thai food, specifically the sweet and sour duck and basil pork belly. We would not recommend going if you are in a hurry because it's a relatively small establishment.

Common Ground ($$+)

Our favorite smash burgers in town. They also serve an amazing focaccia bread as an appetizer, and it's such a large helping that it makes it difficult for us to finish our entrees.

Sunda ($$$)

Sunda is a fancier option for those who like all kinds of Asian food. We love going here for special occasions. Their wagyu crispy rice is out of this world and we're certain that they have mastered the art of the boa bun.

888 ($$$)

This is the fanciest option we're gonna recommend, only because we literally fantasize about it frequently. 888 serves Japanese cuisine, and they have the best sushi in all of Nashville, hands down. They're also a vinyl bar and play different records every night. Definitely get a reservation early if you plan on going!
