Activities

Nashville Zoo

An amazing experience, especially when the sun is out. Defintely visit the Rhinos, Panther, Lemurs, and Flamingos!

Centennial Park

Nashville's own central park... but with a Roman building right in the middle. Be sure to admire the Parthenon if you decide to visit!

Ryman Auditorium

A great venue to see a show in Nashville! They have shows almost every night. Even if you don’t know the artist, the chances are it will be a great show.

Adventure Science Center

This place holds a special spot in Michael's childhood. Honestly, this place is still super fun, even as an adult.

Broadway

Need we say more?

Good Eats

Benji’s Bagels ($+, West Nash)

Amazing bagel shop with many different flavors of Schmears and Bagels to choose from. Definetly a nice breakfast or lunch spot to get something to tie you over.

Hattie B’s ($+, multiple)

Most out-of-towners go to Hattie B’s for authentic Nashville Hot Chicken... and there's a reason why!

Martin’s BBQ Joint ($$, multiple)

One of our favorite BBQ places! Chloe recommends their Brisket Tacos, and Michael recommends (trust me) their Bologna Sandwich if you’re feeling adventurous.

Pancho & Lefty's ($$, South & East Nash)

Our Mexican recommendation. We each get their 3 Taco Combo. Chloe recommends the Hot Chicken and Adobo Chicken Tacos, and Michael recommends the Pork Belly and Avocado Tacos.

Edley's BBQ ($$, multiple)

Our other favorite BBQ place. We both recommend their Burnt Ends with Macaroni n' Cheese.

Thai Ni Yom ($$+, West Nash)

This is our FAVORITE restaurant! We eat there so regularly that the server knows our orders. They serve delicious Thai food (specifically the Duck and Pork Belly), but we would not recommend going if you are in a hurry because it's a relatively small establishment.

Common Ground ($$+, multiple)

Our favorite Smash Burgers in town! They also serve an amazing Focaccia Bread as an appetizer, and it's such a large helping that it makes it difficult for us to finish our entrees.

Sunda ($$$, Downtown Nash)

Sunda is a fancier option for those who REALLY like Thai food. We love going here for special occasions. Their crispy, Wagyu Sushi is out of this world and we're certain that they have mastered the art of the Boa Bun.

888 ($$$, Downtown Nash)

This is the fanciest option we're gonna recommend, only because we literally fantasize about it frequently. 888 is another Thai food place (notice a trend?), but they literally have the best Sushi and Thai food we've ever had (and we've tried many Thai places all over the country). Definetly get a reservation early if you plan on going!
