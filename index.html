Our Story(s)

Chloe’s Version:

It was Halloween night of our sophomore year of college, and my roommate (and now maid of honor), Rachel, had been out of town all weekend. Having missed all of the Halloween costumes and parties, we needed a fun little activity to get us back in the spirit. I used string and balloons to make the two of us green and purple grape costumes and asked one of my friends, Hayden, to pass candy out at his apartment complex for some trick or treating!

One of the places we stopped while trick or treating turned out to be Michael’s apartment. Michael had been a great sport and even put his Halloween costume back onfor the occasion! He was dressed as and impersonating “a drunk uncle from New Jersey”. This included a wife beater, a beer, and an accent. Shockingly, I did not realize this man would be my future husband upon our first meeting. As I was a large bundle of purple grapes, I am sure the feeling was mutual.

Michael and I met again a few weeks later on my 21st birthday, and I was relieved to find the accent and wife beater gone! We chatted all night, became friends, and went on our first date a few months later. We have since experienced long distance, a cross-country move, and lots of adventures together. Halloween is now a special memory for us, and we try to make the holiday fun each year!

Michael’s Version:

What's crazy is it wasn't even Halloween night when we first met. In fact, it was a few weeks before Halloween when Chloe and I first met (although, she seems to have no memory of this). There was a party at Hayden and Matthew's apartment when Chloe and her roommate Rachel (Hayden's previous girlfriend, I'll add) showed up.

The first thing I noticed was Chloe's big, poofy blonde hair, which was shortly followed by her stunning blue eyes. I couldn't tell at the time, but my eyes were wide and my mouth was definetly wide open.

Thankfully, Rachel broke the silence, introduced me as Big Mike, and the rest is history... as long as you fast forward a few weeks to the drunk uncle "experience". I'm certain that my impeccable impression of what a future with me would look like is what sold the deal.

It's true, it was a few months before I officially asked Chloe on a date. Apparently, all of our outings together before that were just as friends... but, I am happy to say that I successfully broke out of the "friend zone" and get to not only call her my best friend, but soon, my wife. I'm so freaking excited for all of the long drives, rainy hikes, new places, and endless adventures we're going to continue having together!
